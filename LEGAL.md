# Legal status and scope — rocky-desktop-tamagotchi

Independent fan-made desktop tamagotchi, not affiliated with Andy Weir, Amazon MGM Studios.

## MIT scope

MIT covers original code, docs, procedural placeholder, abstract icon. Does NOT grant rights in third-party names, characters, designs, trademarks, publicity, promotional materials, or user private assets. Dependencies keep their licenses.

## Excluded / restricted

No redistribution license found for official promotional Rocky STL/GLB archive. Film-derived GLB, fallback PNG, screenshots of Rocky likeness, release ZIPs are included for personal non-commercial demo only in this repo. Not MIT, not for commercial redistribution.

For 100% MIT-safe build, remove those assets and use generic mode.

This is not legal advice.
