🎮 How to play on Mac — visible in 10 seconds

1️⃣
Download & open
Get the ZIP from Releases, move .app to Applications, run the xattr command if Gatekeeper blocks, then open.
2️⃣
Drag anywhere on torso
Grab Rocky's body and move him. Works across multiple monitors. Position is remembered.
3️⃣
Click torso for surprise
Short click = Movie Goodbye 👋 or Knock ✊ (random, viewer-facing). Drag never triggers an action.
4️⃣
Ignore limbs
Only torso is interactive. Click on arm/shadow/transparent → clicks the app behind — no accidental clicks.
5️⃣
Right-click menu
Walk / Quit. Walk moves the actual window like he's marching.
